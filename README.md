# MoodTracker
A personal wellness companion that helps you stay in tune with your emotions.
Log your daily mood, reflect on your thoughts, and track your emotional patterns over time.
Built with PHP, MariaDB, and HTML/CSS, no frameworks used.

## Description
MoodTracker is a personal wellness web application hosted on a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W.
Designed for general users, MoodTracker provides a simple and welcoming space to record
your daily emotional state. Whether you're feeling on top of the world or just getting
through the day, MoodTracker lets you capture that moment with a mood rating and a personal
note. Over time, your entries become a meaningful journal that reflects your emotional
journey, helping you better understand yourself and your patterns.

## Features
- Submit a daily mood rating (1–5) with a note
- View all past mood entries
- Edit or delete entries
- Member introduction page

## Tech Stack
- Hardware: Raspberry Pi Zero 2W
- HTTP Server: Apache
- Backend: PHP (no frameworks)
- Database: MariaDB
- Frontend: HTML, CSS (no frameworks)
